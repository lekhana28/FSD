About The College

Sasi Institute of Technology and Engineering (SITE) is a reputed private autonomous engineering college established in 2002. It is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada and approved by AICTE. SITE offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma programs in engineering, management and technology.

      The Placement Cell at SITE conducts pre-placement training, guest lectures and industry interactions to enhance opportunities.
